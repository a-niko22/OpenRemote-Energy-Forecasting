\section{Conclusion}

EPF stands to be one of the most substantial uses of Artificial Intelligence for companies who's energy used can be timed, and therefore optimised.
This research will contribute to the validation of existing methods within the literature, by expanding on their use case and exposing them to a new set of features. We will also test methods used in other domains,
proposing that adapting them to EPF might be beneficial. Lastly, by comparing similar methods in a methodical and modular way, we may draw conclusions on what methods in particular better or worsen the performance of a particular model.
By doing so, we may find a model architecture that delivers on the accuracy and lag-prediction requested by OpenRemote.
