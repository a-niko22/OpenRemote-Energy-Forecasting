EDA Interactive Charts

Interactive Plotly outputs generated from eda/eda.py. Each page contains its own explanation of what the chart is, which dataset columns it uses, how to read it, and why it matters.

  • Hourly Price Series
    Interactive view of the full hourly EPEX-style target series used for the aligned exogenous analysis.
  • 2025 Quarter-hour Price Series
    Interactive view of the higher-resolution 2025 target segment.
  • Weather Time Series
    Interactive daily-mean weather series from the selected weather source.
  • Production and Load Time Series
    Interactive daily-mean system series for demand and forecasted generation.
  • Correlation Heatmap
    Interactive correlation matrix for price, exogenous variables, and calendar controls.
  • Lagged Correlations
    Interactive lag scan showing how each exogenous variable correlates with price across prior hours.
  • Missingness Over Time
    Interactive daily view of how many exogenous values are missing in the hourly aligned panel.
  • Price vs Selected Exogenous Variables
    Interactive faceted scatter view for the strongest exploratory exogenous candidates.
  • Weather R²: Price vs Total Load
    Interactive grouped-bar comparison of pairwise weather OLS R² against price and total load.
  • Regression Actual vs Predicted
    Interactive diagnostic view for the strongest exploratory OLS model in the EDA.
