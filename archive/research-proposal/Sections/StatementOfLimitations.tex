\section{Statement of Limitations}

This research is limited by multiple factors, such as time that the members can put into the development of the research, as well as the time-window that it needs to be done. Furthermore, the experience of the members
of this research group is being actively built on this very project. Therefore, its execution may contain flaws in methodology.
Hardware limitations are also to be considered, as complex models require better hardware to be properly tested, with the biggest data input as it could be otherwise.
This research will attempt to rank the proposed methods in an attempt to find a good match for EPF, and help the students working on the project learn and practice the scope of the subject.
